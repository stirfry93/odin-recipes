Lucky Lunch

"A special little meal."

This is a fresh, tasty meal for those days you're down on your luck. When you eat a Lucky Lunch, you will find yourself attracting good fortune at every turn.

Ingredients

  • Sea cucumber
  • Tortilla
  • Blue jazz flower

Steps

  1. Upgrade farmhouse to access kitchen
  2. Be sure the necessary ingredients are in your inventory or in the fridge
  3. Access stove and craft dish!
