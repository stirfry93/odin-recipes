Complete Breakfast

"You'll feel ready to take on the world!"

Enjoy this plate of warm, syrupy pancakes, crispy, fried hashbrowns, and a fresh, sunny-side-up egg. This is the perfect meal to get your day started. Whether your day consists of battling cave monsters or playing pool in the town saloon, the Complete Breakfast will give you the long-lasting energy you need.

Ingredients

  • Fried egg
  • Milk
  • Hashbrowns
  • Pancakes

Steps

  1. Upgrade farmhouse to access kitchen
  2. Be sure the necessary ingredients are in your inventory or in the fridge
  3. Access stove and craft dish!
