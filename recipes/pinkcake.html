Pink Cake

"There's little heart candies on top."

This is a light, sweet, and fruity cake for special occasions or the everyday! You will enjoy this if you like a delicate melon flavor and all things nice!

Ingredients

  • Melon
  • Wheat flower
  • Sugar
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Upgrade farmhouse to access kitchen
  2. Be sure the necessary ingredients are in your inventory or in the fridge
  3. Access stove and craft dish!
